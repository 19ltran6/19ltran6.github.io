Interactive Story - Waffles

This story revolves around you buying a gift for your friend's birthday. At the store, you meet this strange man who claims to be the Waffle God. Will you believe him, or continue shopping for your friend's birthday gift?


In this project, I learned how loops worked and how to program 'while' and 'if' statements that flowed and connected to each other. It was difficult managing all the different endings for each scene, so organization was key to making this story understandable. I did make some errors and infinite loops, but I easily fixed it by making my variables more clear and separate. I specifically remember this one story that had a funny plot that involved the normal life of the reader. It was very interactive, had lots of different scenes, and at the end of each story, it gave the option to restart the story so the reader could try a different path. I thought it was pretty cool that the whole story was a running loop.
